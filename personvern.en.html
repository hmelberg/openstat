Privacy Statement

Microdata Script Runner is a free, public version with no login and no accounts.

Last updated: 2026-07-06.

1. What is this?

Microdata Script Runner is a hobby project run by Hans Olav Melberg that helps users write and run code against microdata.no syntax, Python, and R directly in the browser. This version requires no login and no account. Your script, any datasets you load, and the run results stay in your browser — they are never sent to any server unless you choose to use the AI assistant.

2. What happens when you use the AI assistant?

The AI features (code suggestions, interpreting results, data-minimization assessment, web search) require you to paste your own Anthropic API key under AI settings. When you do:

  • The key is stored only locally in your browser (localStorage) and sent as a header (X-Anthropic-Key) to our server, which forwards it directly to Anthropic for that one request. We do not store or log the key.
  • The question and script excerpt you send is forwarded to Anthropic to generate a response. We do not store the content of these requests on our own server.
  • Your IP address is used briefly for simple rate limiting to prevent abuse, and is not retained.
  • Usage (tokens/cost) is billed to your own Anthropic account — we do not see or store payment information.

3. Cookies and local storage

The app only uses localStorage in your own browser to remember your settings (e.g. Anthropic key, chosen mode, theme). None of this is sent to us unless you actively use the AI features.

4. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell or rent out data. When you use the AI features, your question/script is shared with:

  • Anthropic (AI service, USA) — to generate the response
  • Netlify (hosting for the app and server functions)

5. Your rights

Since we do not store any personal data (no account, no server-side content storage), there is normally nothing to request access to or deletion of. If you have questions, please get in touch (see below). You may also file a complaint with the Norwegian Data Protection Authority (datatilsynet.no).
