* Example: Stata – linear regression (OLS)
* The first block imports variables via microdata.
* The second block runs a simple OLS regression of income on sex.
* Read the coefficient for kjonn: positive means higher income for the group.

#micro
require no.ssb.fdb:54 as fd
create-dataset folk
import fd/BEFOLKNING_KJOENN as kjonn
import fd/INNTEKT_WLONN 2022-01-01 as inntekt

#stata
summarize inntekt
regress inntekt kjonn
